细分类近几年顶会文章

距离上次更新博客已经快一年了啊喂！平常图方便总是把笔记写在印象笔记里，好不容易空闲下来，是时候把印象笔记的里的东西誊到博客上了~
先罗列一下方法较为中规中矩的一些论文吧~ 跨度2013年至2017年，并不全面，只是一个参考~

2013

  • 《Symbiotic Segmentation and Part Localization for Fine-Grained Categorization》
    Author: Yuning Chai, Victor Lempitsky, Andrew Zisserman
    Information: ICCV 2013

  • 《Hierarchical Part Matching for Fine-Grained Visual Categorization》
    Author: Lingxi Xie, Qi Tian, Richang Hong, Shuicheng Yan, Bo Zhang
    Information: ICCV 2013

2014

  • 《Bird Species Categorization Using Pose Normalized Deep Convolutional Nets》
    Author: Steve Branson, Grant Van Horn, Serge Belongie, Pietro Perona
    Information: CVPR 2014

  • 《Part-based R-CNNs for Fine-grained Category Detection》
    Author: Ning Zhang, Jeff Donahue, Ross Girshick, Trevor Darrell
    Information: ECCV 2014

2015

  • 《Bilinear CNN Models for Fine-grained Visual Recognition》
    Author: Tsung-Yu Lin, Aruni RoyChowdhury, Subhransu Maji
    Information: ICCV 2015

  • 《Fine-Grained Recognition without Part Annotations》
    Author: Jonathan Krause, Hailin Jin, Jianchao Yang, Li Fei-Fei
    Information: CVPR 2015

  • Deep LAC: Deep Localization, Alignment and Classification for Fine-grained Recognition》
    Author: Di Lin, Xiaoyong Shen, Cewu Lu, Jiaya Jia
    Information: CVPR 2015

  • 《Neural Activation Constellations: Unsupervised Part Model Discovery with Convolutional Networks》
    Author: Marcel Simon, Erik Rodner
    Information: ICCV 2015

  • 《Multiple Granularity Descriptors for Fine-grained Categorization》
    Author: Dequan Wang, Zhiqiang Shen, Jie Shao, Wei Zhang, Xiangyang Xue and Zheng Zhang
    Information: ICCV 2015

  • 《The Application of Two-level Attention Models in Deep Convolutional Neural
    Network for Fine-grained Image Classification》
    Author: Tianjun Xiao, Yichong Xu, Kuiyuan Yang, Jiaxing Zhang, Yuxin Peng, Zheng Zhang
    Information: CVPR 2015《The Application of Two-level Attention Models in Deep Convolutional Neural
    Network for Fine-grained Image Classification》
    Author: Tianjun Xiao, Yichong Xu, Kuiyuan Yang, Jiaxing Zhang, Yuxin Peng, Zheng Zhang
    Information: CVPR 2015

2016

  • 《Mask-CNN: Localizing Parts and Selecting Descriptors for Fine-Grained Image Recognition》
    Author: Xiu-Shen Wei, Chen-Wei Xie, JianxinWu
    Information: CVPR 2016

  • 《Picking Deep Filter Responses for Fine-grained Image Recognition》
    Author: Xiaopeng Zhang, Hongkai Xiong, Wengang Zhou, Weiyao Lin, Qi Tian
    Information: CVPR 2016

  • SPDA-CNN: Unifying Semantic Part Detection and Abstraction for Fine-grained Recognition》
    Author: Han Zhang, Tao Xu, Mohamed Elhoseiny, Xiaolei Huang, Shaoting Zhang, Ahmed Elgammal, Dimitris Metaxas
    Information: CVPR 2016

  • 《Learning Deep Features for Discriminative Localization》
    Author: Bolei Zhou, Aditya Khosla, Agata Lapedriza, Aude Oliva, Antonio Torralba
    Information: CVPR 2016

2017

  • 《Fully Convolutional Attention Networks for Fine-Grained Recognition》
    Author: Xiao Liu, Tian Xia, Jiang Wang, Yi Yang, Feng Zhou, Yuanqing Lin
    Information: CVPR 2017

  • 《Diversified Visual Attention Networks for Fine-Grained Object Classification》
    Author: Bo Zhao, Xiao Wu
    Information: IEEE TRANSACTION ON MULTIMEDIA 2017

  • 《Weakly Supervised Learning of Part Selection Model with Spatial Constraints for Fine-Grained Image Classification》
    Author: Xiangteng He, Yuxin Peng
    Information: AAAI 2017

  • 《Fine-grained Image Classification via Combining Vision and Language》
    Author: Xiangteng He and Yuxin Peng
    Information: CVPR 2017

  • 《Fine-Grained Recognition as HSnet Search for Informative Image Parts》
    Author: Michael Lam, Behrooz Mahasseni, Sinisa Todorovic
    Information: CVPR 2017

  • 《Look Closer to See Better: Recurrent Attention Convolutional Neural Network for Fine-grained Image Recognition》
    Author: Jianlong Fu, Heliang Zheng, TaoMei
    Information: CVPR 2017

  • 《Learning Multi-Attention Convolutional Neural Network for Fine-Grained Image Recognition》
    Author: Heliang Zheng1, Jianlong Fu, TaoMei, Jiebo Luo
    Information: ICCV 2017

先总结到这里吧~ 下次再来整理论文读书笔记~
